Privacy Policy for Spread Heads

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

Avant Real Estate Investments LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Spread Heads mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication
  • Username and display name - Your chosen identifier in the app
  • Password - Securely encrypted and stored

Usage Information

We collect information about your use of the App:

  • Game picks - Your predictions for NFL games (spreads and totals)
  • League data - Leagues you create or join, league settings, and member information
  • Performance statistics - Your wins, losses, and other game-related metrics
  • App activity - Features you use and how you interact with the App

Device Information

  • Push notification tokens - To send you notifications (if enabled)
  • Device identifiers - For app functionality and crash reporting

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and track your game picks and standings
  • Enable league features and social interactions
  • Send push notifications about game times, results, and league activity (if enabled)
  • Calculate leaderboards, statistics, and achievements
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Information Sharing

We share your information in the following circumstances:

Within Leagues

  • Your username, display name, picks, and statistics are visible to other members of leagues you join
  • League creators can see member information within their leagues

Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate our App:

  • Supabase - Authentication and database hosting
  • Expo - App development platform and push notifications
  • ESPN API - NFL game data and scores

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update your information - Edit your profile and account settings in the App
  • Delete your account - Request permanent deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of notifications - Disable push notifications in app settings
  • Leave leagues - Remove yourself from any league at any time

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, when appropriate, through in-app notifications or email.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as ESPN). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.
